Why ARIMA Lags

As seen in the charts above, the ARIMA model relies on linear, historical averages. When the gold market experiences sudden geopolitical shocks or rapid inflation, ARIMA struggles to adapt quickly. This results in statistical predictions that noticeably lag behind the actual real-world market volatility.

The LSTM Advantage

Conversely, the Deep LSTM network utilizes advanced gating mechanisms to "remember" long-term dependencies and "forget" irrelevant noise. This allows the neural network to closely hug the actual market line, successfully adapting to complex, non-linear market behaviors with significantly higher forecasting accuracy.

Indian Market: Verifying Gold Genuity

To ensure physical gold purity in India, always check for the BIS Hallmark before making any real-world purchase. It consists of three distinct, legally required signs:

1. BIS Logo

A distinct triangle logo identifying the Bureau of Indian Standards certification.

2. Purity Grade

The metric identifier. For example, '22K916' means 22 Karat (exactly 91.6% pure gold).

3. HUID Code

A unique 6-digit alphanumeric code laser-engraved. Verifiable via the BIS Care App.
